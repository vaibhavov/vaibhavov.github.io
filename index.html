About Me

Hola! I'm Vaibhav Sonkar, a Master's student at IIT Bombay, specializing in Geoinformatics and Natural Resources Engineering. My academic journey bridges civil engineering foundations with cutting-edge research in geospatial technologies, deep learning, and AI.

Currently, I'm working on "High-Performance Onboard Data Processing" at the Centre of Studies in Resources Engineering (CSRE), IIT Bombay, under the guidance of Prof. Surya S. Durbha. This project aligns with my broader interest in building scalable, real-time AI models for satellite-based disaster mapping and Earth observation.

My technical expertise spans data structures and algorithms, deep learning, machine learning, GIS, and remote sensing. I thrive at the intersection of domain science and intelligent systems, particularly in transforming raw spatial data into actionable insights.

Academically, I’ve been fortunate to achieve All India Rank 1 in GATE Geomatics Engineering, 2024. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Civil Engineering from IIT Roorkee, where I built a solid foundation in transportation systems, surveying, and hydrological modeling.

Beyond academics, I enjoy exploring culinary arts, singing, and crafting things hands-on, be it DIY projects.

Interests

Software Development

I’m passionate about software development and enjoy building scalable, real-world solutions. My strong foundation in Data Structures and Algorithms helps me write optimized and efficient code, and I constantly explore new tools and frameworks to enhance my development skills.

Deep Learning

I’m deeply interested in deep learning and its ability to solve complex real-world problems. I enjoy building models for tasks like image recognition, classification, segmentation, and anomaly detection. I constantly explore new architectures and research to stay updated in the field.

Geospatial Applications

I am always astonished by the diverse applications of geospatial technologies and their role in solving environmental and societal challenges.
