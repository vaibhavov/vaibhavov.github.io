About Me

Hola! I'm Vaibhav Sonkar, a gripped Master's student at IIT Bombay, specializing in Geoinformatics and Natural Resources Engineering. My academic journey bridges civil engineering foundations with cutting-edge research in geospatial technologies, deep learning, and AI.

Currently, I'm working on "High-Performance Onboard Data Processing" at the Centre of Studies in Resources Engineering (CSRE), IIT Bombay, under the guidance of Prof. Surya S. Durbha. This project aligns with my broader interest in building scalable, real-time AI models for satellite-based disaster mapping and Earth observation.

My technical expertise spans data structures and algorithms, deep learning, machine learning, GIS, and remote sensing. I thrive at the intersection of domain science and intelligent systems, particularly in transforming raw spatial data into actionable insights.

Academically, I’ve been fortunate to achieve All India Rank 1 in GATE Geomatics Engineering, 2024. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Civil Engineering from IIT Roorkee, where I built a solid foundation in transportation systems, surveying, and hydrological modeling.

Beyond academics, I enjoy exploring culinary arts, singing to unwind, and crafting things hands-on, be it DIY projects.
